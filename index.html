Introduction: 

Tangail Polytechnic Institute is the newest Polytechnic Institute in Bangladesh. It was established in 1991 with only 40 students in the first your classes of Diploma-In_Engineering in one technology, named, Computer with the growing demands of mid-level technical manpower home and abroad the Institute has since greatly expended. The Institute now offers courses in four technologys.


Computer Technology
Civil Technology
Electronics Technology
RAC Technology

Operation: 

Tangail Polytechnic Institute operates under the executive control of the ministry of education (Directorate of Technical Education). The academic program's, however, function under the regulation of the Bangladesh Technical Education Board, Agargaon, Sher-e-Bangla Nagar Dhaka - 1225. The main campus includes of four - storied building, two large workshop building which are accommodating the Class rooms, Office, Library, Workshop & Laboratories and one 200 capacity Auditorium.

Location: 

The Institute is located at Fokirtola, Tangail about two kilometers from the city of Tangail (towards south) and on the west side of the big and mighty river jamuna. It how over covers an area of about 3 acres of land.
